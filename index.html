How to use the site:
  • Clicking Collection Information in the top left will show the number of records in the collection and the value.
  • Click on any image for more information on the record.
  • In the popup will be information on the label, year released. If available it will also include a youtube video and the price of the lowest available copy on discogs (clicking on the price will take you directly to discogs)
  • Clicking on the title will bring up the record in Discogs
About the site:
  • A one page site created primarily with bootstrap
  • Information is pulled from Discogs via api and rendered dynamically on the page.
  • Once a tab has loaded once it will not be loaded again until the page is refreshed.
